- Core methodology for reaction enumeration
- SMARTS pattern implementation

**Key Contributions:**
- Established standard methodology for Reaction SMARTS enumeration
- Provides N-acylation to amide reaction (1165 examples) - directly applicable to peptide coupling
- No protection/deprotection steps (deemed unnecessary for in silico work)
